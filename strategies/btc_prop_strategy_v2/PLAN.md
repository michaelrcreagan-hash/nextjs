## Deviations from DISCOVERY.md (all data-driven, not design changes)

1. **Timeframes:** Trend Rider spec'd 4H entry/1D trend; Range Hunter 1H. Only daily bars exist -- both run on 1D. Signal frequencies will differ from spec; treat baseline as directional evidence, not final calibration.
2. **Funding Farmer:** no funding-rate data -- module stubbed, produces zero trades, 20% allocation renormalized across the other two (Trend Rider 62.5% / Range Hunter 37.5%) for the backtest only.
3. **Scale-out 50/30/20:** simplified to single TP at rr_ratio + trailing/stop exits for the baseline backtester. Partial exits added in a later iteration.
4. **Range Hunter validation closes (3 daily closes):** skipped -- on daily bars that's a 3-day lag on a 1-7 day mean-reversion hold, kills the trade. Volume confirmation gate kept.
5. **Confluence scorer:** macro/on-chain/derivatives layers unscored (no data). Technical + regime (365DMA) layers only.
6. **Look-ahead control:** signals computed on close of day t, executed at open of day t+1.
